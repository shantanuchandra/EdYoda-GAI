# Lumière Bakery — Policy and Operations Manual

This document sets out the complete operational policies for Lumière Artisan Bakery. It applies to all customer orders, catering engagements, online transactions, franchise partnerships, and staff interactions. Policies are effective from the date of publication and supersede all prior versions.

---

## Cancellation Policy [Cancellation] [Refunds]

Cancellation entitlement depends on when the customer notifies Lumière, measured from the scheduled pickup or delivery time.

- **72 hours or more before pickup/delivery:** Full refund issued, no deduction.
- **48–72 hours before pickup/delivery:** 50% refund; the remaining 50% is retained by Lumière as a non-recoverable preparation and handling fee.
- **Under 48 hours before pickup/delivery:** No refund. The full order value is forfeited.
- **Custom cakes:** No refund is available once baking has begun. Lumière's facilitation team will notify the customer at least 24 hours before baking starts so the customer can still cancel before that point.
- **Force majeure** (including power outages, severe weather, supply chain disruption, or natural disaster attributable to Lumière): The customer may choose a full reschedule to a future date or a full refund, at their discretion.

---

## Refund Process [Refunds] [Payments]

- Approved refunds are processed within **5–7 business days** to the original payment method used at checkout.
- Orders originally paid in **cash** are refunded in cash at the branch during normal trading hours. The customer must present their order reference.
- **No refund is issued for change of mind** on standard baked items once those items have entered the baking stage, regardless of the reason cited.

---

## Order Amendments [Amendments] [Custom Orders]

- **Standard orders:** Amendments (ingredients, quantity, collection time) are accepted up to **12 hours** before the scheduled fulfilment time, subject to availability.
- **Custom cakes — flavour change:** Accepted within **24 hours of the original booking**. No flavour or design changes are permitted after that window.
- **Size upgrades:** Accepted at any point before baking begins, provided production capacity allows.
- **Downsizing:** Not permitted after order confirmation under any circumstances. Customers who wish to reduce their order must cancel and rebook at the applicable cancellation tier.

---

## Delivery Failure Policy [Delivery] [Redelivery] [Perishables]

- If a delivery is attempted and the **customer is absent**, Lumière will make **one re-attempt within 2 hours** of the initial attempt at no additional charge.
- A **second re-delivery attempt**, if required, is charged at **₹60**, payable before re-dispatch.
- Perishable items are held for a maximum of **4 hours post-baking**. After that window, Lumière is not responsible for product quality, safety, or spoilage.
- Customers are encouraged to ensure someone is available to receive the delivery, or to arrange collection from the branch as an alternative.

---

## Catering and Bulk Orders [Catering] [Bulk] [Events] [Minimum Order]

- **Minimum order value:** ₹5,000 for all catering packages.
- **Lead time:** A minimum of **5 business days** is required from booking to fulfilment. Requests with shorter lead times cannot be guaranteed.
- A **signed confirmation form** and a **50% deposit** must be received before the order is placed into production.
- Menu customisation is available but remains subject to the seasonal availability of ingredients at the time of fulfilment. Lumière will notify the customer promptly if a requested item cannot be sourced.
- **Lumière supplies food only.** Crockery, cutlery, serving staff, décor, and event setup are outside the scope of service and must be arranged independently.

---

## Franchise Territory Terms [Franchise] [Territory] [Compliance] [Revenue]

- Each franchise partner is licensed to operate **within their designated territory only**. Cross-territory sales, online or in-person, are expressly prohibited.
- Territory exclusivity is subject to **annual review** based on performance and market conditions.
- If a franchisee's **monthly revenue falls below ₹2,00,000**, the performance review clause in their franchise agreement is automatically triggered, which may result in remedial measures or licence review.
- Franchisees must maintain accurate monthly revenue records and submit them to Lumière corporate on the schedule specified in their agreement.

---

## Allergen Liability [Allergens] [Health] [Safety] [Cross-Contamination]

- Lumière makes all reasonable operational efforts to prevent cross-contamination between products.
- **Lumière cannot guarantee that any product is entirely free from allergens**, including but not limited to gluten, tree nuts, peanuts, dairy, eggs, sesame, and soya, due to shared kitchen equipment and preparation surfaces.
- Customers with a **known or suspected severe allergy must disclose it at the time of ordering**. This disclosure allows Lumière staff to advise on product suitability.
- Lumière is **not liable** for adverse allergic reactions in customers who did not disclose their allergy when placing their order and who proceeded on the basis of their own risk assessment.

## Loyalty Programme Terms [Loyalty] [Lumière Circle] [Points] [Redemption]

- Points are earned at the rate of **1 point per ₹100 spent** on qualifying purchases.
- Points are **valid for 24 months** from the date of the last qualifying transaction on the account.
- Points are **non-transferable** and cannot be moved between customer accounts.
- **Minimum redemption threshold:** 50 points per transaction.
- Points **cannot be combined** with any other promotional offer, discount, or voucher scheme running concurrently.
- In the event of any dispute regarding a loyalty account balance or transaction, **Lumière's decision is final and binding**.

---

## Online Ordering and Payment Terms [Online Orders] [Payments] [UPI] [WhatsApp]

- All orders placed through the Lumière online portal require **full prepayment** at checkout.
- Accepted payment methods: **UPI, net banking, credit card, and debit card**.
- Lumière does **not store, retain, or share** card or banking credentials. Payment processing is handled by a certified third-party gateway.
- An **order confirmation is sent via WhatsApp within 30 minutes** of successful payment.
- If no confirmation is received within 30 minutes, the customer should **call their branch directly** to verify order status before assuming the transaction failed.

---

## Staff and Customer Conduct [Conduct] [Refusal of Service]

- Abusive, threatening, or harassing behaviour directed at any Lumière staff member will result in the customer being **immediately refused service**.
- Lumière reserves the right to **refuse any order without being required to provide an explanation** to the customer or any third party.
- These provisions apply equally to in-store, telephone, and online interactions.
