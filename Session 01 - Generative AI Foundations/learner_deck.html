Topic 01·A Brief History
A Field Guide to Generative AI
A brief history

How AI evolved, in four stages.

  1. Rule-Based 1950s — 1980s

    If-then logic. A vending machine: fixed input, fixed output.

  2. Machine Learning 1990s — 2010s

    Learns patterns from data. A spam filter that gets smarter over time.

  3. Deep Learning 2010s

    Neural networks. The AI that beat human champions at Go.

  4. Foundation Models 2020 — Now

    Trained on the internet. Performs tasks it was never specifically trained for.

Topic 05·The Four Limits
A Field Guide to Generative AI
Four hard limits

Where a single LLM call falls short.

— High risk —

Hallucination

Confident, wrong answers that look factual.

Bites at: citations · names · numbers · dates
— Medium risk —

Static knowledge

Knows nothing after its training cutoff date.

Bites at: current events · new policy · recent prices
— Medium risk —

No memory

Forgets everything between conversations.

Bites at: long projects · repeat clients · handovers
— Limiting —

No action

Can produce text. Cannot actually do things.

Bites at: booking · sending · scheduling · checking
