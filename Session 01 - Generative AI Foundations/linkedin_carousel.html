Plot Twist·The Demo That Refused
A Field Guide to Generative AI
A failed demo is a curriculum update

My most rehearsed demo —
broke. And that's the win.

— What I planned —

Ask ChatGPT about a fake 2019 Indian Supreme Court case to demo hallucination.

I've used this exact prompt for 2 years. It always invented a confident, fully-cited fake judgment. Every time.

— What actually happened —

All 3 models searched the web in real time and refused.

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini — every one of them said: "This case does not exist." The hallucination demo refused to hallucinate.

A failed demo is the best curriculum update. The field outran my deck.

Session 01·Takeaways
A Field Guide to Generative AI
By the numbers

What I'm taking away — from teaching, not teaching about.

60%

Hands-on is the floor.

Anything less and you're delivering a webinar, not a class.

0slides

Open with a demo.

Not a definition. Not a self-intro. A demo earns the next 110 minutes.

3tabs

Compare 3 models live.

Builds intuition faster than any 30-minute lecture on model differences.

1demo

Plan for it to break.

The failure usually teaches more than the script.

1prompt

End with the prompt they'll use tomorrow.

Nothing else compounds. If they don't leave with a tool, they leave with a feeling — and feelings fade by Friday.
