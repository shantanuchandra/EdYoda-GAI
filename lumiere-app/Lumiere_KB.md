## About Lumière [About]

Lumière is a Mumbai-based artisan bakery founded in 2018 by pastry chef Priya Menon, who trained at the Institut Paul Bocuse in Lyon before returning to India. The bakery is celebrated for its French-technique pastries reimagined with Indian flavours — think cardamom croissants, mango-coconut opera cakes, and rose-lychee éclairs alongside classic European staples. With three branches across Mumbai, Lumière has become a trusted name for both everyday indulgences and milestone celebration cakes.

All baking is done fresh daily at each branch using imported European butter and locally sourced seasonal fruit. The brand's philosophy is "no shortcuts, no pre-mixes" — every item on the menu, from the simplest canelé to a five-kilogram fondant wedding cake, is made entirely in-house. Lumière has been featured in Condé Nast Traveller India, Time Out Mumbai, and the Hindustan Times food supplement.

## Allergens & Ingredients [Allergens] [Ingredients] [Dietary]

Lumière takes allergen safety seriously. Please read the following information carefully before ordering.

**Nuts:** Almond croissants contain almonds. Macarons are made with almond flour. **The Lumière kitchen is NOT a nut-free environment.** Nuts are handled daily across all workstations. Cross-contamination is possible for every item produced in the kitchen, including those not listed as containing nuts.

**Eggs:** All standard cake recipes contain eggs. Eggless versions are prepared in separate baking trays; however, they are baked in the same ovens as egg-containing products. Customers with egg allergies should be aware of this shared oven risk.

**Gluten:** All breads and pastries contain wheat gluten. Lumière does **not** offer any gluten-free products at this time.

**Dairy:** All products contain dairy. Butter is a core ingredient across the entire menu. Lumière does **not** offer vegan or dairy-free alternatives.

**Important notice:** Due to shared kitchen equipment and production facilities, **Lumière cannot guarantee that any item is completely free from any allergen.** Customers with severe or life-threatening allergies are strongly advised to consult their doctor before ordering and to exercise personal judgement.
